I've been building software in the open since 2008. Today I write the backend of Classlife Education, an edtech platform in Barcelona: DDD, CQRS and hexagonal architecture in production, not on slides.

Herrero de herramientas. Primero fueron familias de paquetes open source para Zend Framework y Laminas: RBAC con Doctrine, login social OAuth2, APIs CRUD automáticas. Hoy construyo las herramientas que hacen que los agentes de IA trabajen con la disciplina de un equipo senior: forge-methodology encadena spec, grill adversarial, plan y verificación; design-review juzga la interfaz con cuatro lentes en un navegador real; token-economy recorta el coste multiagente sin perder cobertura.

A blacksmith of tools. First came families of open-source packages for Zend Framework and Laminas: RBAC with Doctrine, OAuth2 social login, automatic CRUD APIs. Today I build the tools that make AI agents work with the discipline of a senior team: forge-methodology chains spec, adversarial grill, plan and verify; design-review judges the interface through four lenses in a real browser; token-economy cuts multi-agent cost without losing coverage.
